About RBC Donate

RBC Donate adds value to the company itself by using social media to advertise the bank through donations to non-profit organizations and events. RBC Donate app is for users who are interested in contributing towards a non-profit organization/charity but are unwilling to conduct research, but would readily support the cause if it were presented in a compact and quick access format. Donating to events can be a hassle for both the supporters and the organization. RBC Donate brings it to your Smartphone and Tablet to make it easy to support the people in need.

For more information please visit the RBC's main website.

Feedback or questions: contact@rbcdonate.ca
